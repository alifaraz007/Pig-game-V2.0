GAME RULES:

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
- In each turn, a player rolls dices as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
- BUT, if the player rolls a 1 in any of dices, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The first player to reach 50 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
- And after that if he start new game then previous winning score will be the new winning score
